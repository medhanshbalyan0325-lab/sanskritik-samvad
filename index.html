LOK SABHA

Topic: Caste Census

The Lok Sabha committee will deliberate on the critical issue of caste census, examining its necessity for policy formulation and governance. Participants will explore the social, political, and economic implications of conducting a comprehensive caste census in modern India.

Key discussion points include data accuracy, resource allocation, social justice implications, and the balance between identity politics and inclusive development.

×

RAJYA SABHA

Topic: Exam Scam

This committee addresses the pressing issue of examination irregularities and recruitment scams that have plagued India's education and employment sectors. Members will propose comprehensive reforms to ensure transparency and accountability.

Focus areas include digital security, oversight mechanisms, anti-corruption measures, and rehabilitation of affected candidates.

×

ALL INDIA POLITICAL PARTIES MEET (AIPPM)

Topic: The Place of Worship Act, 1992

A crucial discussion on one of India's most significant legislative acts concerning religious places. The committee will examine whether this act safeguards India's secular fabric or restricts the pursuit of historical justice.

Debates will cover constitutional principles, historical grievances, communal harmony, and the balance between religious rights and social stability.

×

UNITED NATIONS GENERAL ASSEMBLY (UNGA)

Topic: Global Digital Divide

Addressing the critical challenge of ensuring equitable access to AI and emerging technologies for developing nations. This committee will work on innovative solutions to bridge the technological gap that threatens to deepen global inequalities.

Key areas include technology transfer, capacity building, digital infrastructure, and sustainable development through inclusive innovation.

×

UNITED NATIONS SECURITY COUNCIL (UNSC)

Topic: Preventing Cyber Warfare

Establishing international protocols and red lines for digital conflicts between state actors. This high-stakes committee will draft frameworks for cyber-security governance and digital warfare prevention.

Discussions will encompass international law, digital sovereignty, critical infrastructure protection, and diplomatic solutions to cyber conflicts.

×

INTERNATIONAL PRESS

Focus: Journalism, Photography & Caricature

The fourth estate in action! International Press members will document, analyze, and present global perspectives on all committee proceedings through comprehensive journalism, impactful photography, and thought-provoking caricatures.

This dynamic committee combines traditional reporting with creative visual storytelling to bring parliamentary proceedings to life.
